Josef Ginnerskov

Josef Ginnerskov is a postdoctoral researcher in analytical sociology and a junior fellow at The Swedish Excellence Centre for Computational Social Science (SweCSS) at Linköping University.

Mette Ginnerskov-Dahlberg

Mette Ginnerskov-Dahlberg is Associate Professor (docent) in Sociology of Education at Uppsala University. She specializes in the lived experience of migration and the labour market integration of student migrants.
